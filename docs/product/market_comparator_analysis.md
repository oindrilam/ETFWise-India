# Phase 1A - Market Comparator Analysis

## Purpose

This document compares existing ETF comparison and investment research platforms with ETFWise India.

The goal is not to copy existing products. The goal is to understand what users already get from the market and define how ETFWise India will be different.

ETFWise India will focus on beginner-friendly, source-backed, educational ETF comparison using official/public data sources.

## Important Rule

Do not copy exact text, tables, logos, screenshots, or UI design from any competitor.

The app can learn from market gaps, but all wording, explanations, UI, and comparison logic must be original.

## Market Gap

Many ETF platforms show data, tables, filters, or fund pages, but beginners may still struggle to understand:

- what ETF means
- how ETF differs from mutual fund/index fund
- why expense ratio matters
- why tracking error matters
- why liquidity/volume matters
- why NAV and market price can differ
- how to compare two ETFs without receiving investment advice

## ETFWise India Unique Value Proposition

ETFWise India is an educational AI ETF comparison assistant for Indian ETFs. It helps users compare ETFs using official/public data sources and explains the meaning of ETF metrics in simple language.

It does not give buy/sell recommendations. It does not provide personalized investment advice.

| Android app | Improves accessibility. | Later |

## MVP Positioning

ETFWise India will be positioned as:

"Compare Indian ETFs with official-source-backed data and beginner-friendly AI explanations."

It will not be positioned as:

- "Best ETF to buy"
- "Trading signal app"
- "Personal investment advisor"
- "Guaranteed return recommender"

## User Personas

### 1. Beginner investor

Needs simple explanations of ETF terms.

### 2. MBA / finance learner

Needs structured comparison and concept clarity.

### 3. Long-term passive investor

Needs cost, tracking, and liquidity comparison.

### 4. Product interview reviewer

Needs to see clear product thinking, data design, and safe AI design.

## Example User Questions

- What is an ETF?
- Compare two Nifty 50 ETFs.
- Which ETF has lower expense ratio?
- Why does tracking error matter?
- Why is ETF volume important?
- What does NAV mean?
- Why is market price different from NAV?
- Which data is missing for this ETF?
- Explain this comparison like I am a beginner.

## Non-Goals

ETFWise India will not:

- give buy/sell calls
- execute trades
- connect to broker accounts
- promise returns
- provide personalized financial advice
- scrape private or restricted data
- copy third-party UI or content
- use third-party logos without permission
